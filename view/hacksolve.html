Hack 'N' Solve

Hack'n'Solve, the third and final part of the triathlon, is a 36 Hours Virtual Hackathon where we aim to promote Hackers and help them nurture their new ideas and prototypes to solve one or more of the 17 sustainable development goals by the United Nations.

Rules 🔧

  • There will be 2 to 4 people in one team.
  • The theme for the event will be the UN's 17 Sustainable Development Goals.
  • Deadline to Register: 11th March 2022
  • Round 1 will end at 12:00pm on 13th March 2022
  • 10 shortlisted teams will be invited to give a live demonstration in front of the judges later in the evening on 13th March 2022.
  • Judging Criteria will be theme, presentation, usage of Google technologies, aesthetic, completeness, accuracy, user experience and creativity.

Theme 🌟

The Sustainable Development Goals or Global Goals are a collection of 17 interlinked global goals designed to be a "blueprint to achieve a better and more sustainable future for all". The SDGs were set up in 2015 by the United Nations General Assembly and are intended to be achieved by the year 2030.
