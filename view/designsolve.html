Design 'N' Solve

A Designathon you have never seen before. Packed with mentorship sessions, mini events and a lot of prizes up for grabs, Devcation's Design 'n' Solve will offer you a chance to learn and showcase your designing skills.

Rules 🔧

  • There will be 2 to 4 people in one team.
  • The theme for the event will be the UN's 17 Sustainable Development Goals.
  • Deadline to Register: 27th February 2022
  • An email with the entire structure of the designathon will be sent to the registered teams by 28th February 2022
  • Teams will be invited on a sync call with the mentor assigned to them on 02nd March 2022.
  • There will be 2 mini-events on 03rd and 04th March 2022 for which separate prizes will be awarded to the winners.
  • Final submission deadline is 11:59 PM on 05th March 2022.
  • The judging criteria will be creativity, novelty, features, ease of implementation, aesthetic and its contribution and relevance to UN's 17 SDGs.

Theme 🌟

The Sustainable Development Goals or Global Goals are a collection of 17 interlinked global goals designed to be a "blueprint to achieve a better and more sustainable future for all". The SDGs were set up in 2015 by the United Nations General Assembly and are intended to be achieved by the year 2030.
